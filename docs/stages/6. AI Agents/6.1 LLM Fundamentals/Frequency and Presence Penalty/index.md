# Frequency and Presence Penalty

## Purpose

Explain how penalties reduce repetition or encourage new topics, and why that
can be risky for agents.

## Contributor Scope

| Control | Beginner meaning |
|---|---|
| Frequency penalty | discourages words that appear many times |
| Presence penalty | discourages words once they have appeared at all |

## Build

Compare a long creative response and a structured JSON response with penalties
turned off and on.

## Exit Criteria

You can explain why penalties may help creative writing but harm structured
tool outputs.
